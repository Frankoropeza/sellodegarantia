## 1. SELLO LISO DE COLOR (Pigmented PVC Shrink Bands)

### Technical Specifications
- **Material:** PVC termoencogible pigmentado en masa (pigment is mixed during extrusion, NOT surface-applied ink)
- **Calibres (thickness):** 40, 50, 60 micras (1.6 - 2.4 mil)
  - 40 micras: standard for neck bands on regular bottles
  - 50 micras: intermediate for irregular cap shapes
  - 60 micras: full-body seals and garrafones
- **Diameters available:** 20mm to 300mm (industry-wide)
- **Shrinkage:** 55-65% transversal (cross-direction), 10-15% longitudinal
- **Shrinkage onset temperature:** 60°C; optimal: 95-100°C
- **Application temperature:** 90°C - 130°C depending on gauge and line speed
- **Standard colors:** Rojo (red), azul (blue), verde (green), dorado (gold), negro (black)
- **Custom colors:** Any Pantone Coated (PMS) reference, requires 10-12 business days
- **Finish options:** Opaque or semi-transparent depending on pigment load
- **Food grade:** Yes — pigments approved for indirect food contact

### Key Benefits & Selling Points
1. **Color is in the mass, not on the surface** — won't scratch, fade, smudge, or wash off. Unlike printed seals, the color survives humidity, friction, and chemical exposure.
2. **SKU differentiation without printing cost** — 4 flavors = 4 colors, identifiable at a glance on the production line, warehouse, and point of sale without opening the box.
3. **Security communication** — Red or black visibly signals "sealed, not opened" faster than transparent. Critical for pharma OTC and supplements.
4. **Brand reinforcement without rotogravure** — Match exact Pantone corporate color (e.g., Pantone 485 = Coca-Cola red) without paying for engraving cylinders.
5. **UV light filtering** — Pigmented PVC provides partial protection from UV degradation for light-sensitive contents (amber/gold tones especially).
6. **No minimum for standard colors** — 5,000 pieces per color vs. 10,000 for printed seals.

### Target Industries
- Beverage bottlers with multiple flavors/SKUs
- Pharmaceutical OTC labs differentiating product lines
- Cosmetics with tiered product lines (regular vs. premium vs. organic)
- Purified water distributors (blue for standard, gold for premium)
- Honey, sauces, and artisanal food producers seeking shelf differentiation

### Common Applications
- Flavor differentiation on juice, kombucha, sports drink lines
- Color-coding pharmaceutical product families (red = pain, blue = cold, green = digestive)
- Premium presentation for cosmetics (gold/black convey luxury)
- Water purification companies with branded garrafones

## 2. SELLO IMPRESO A 6 TINTAS (Rotogravure Printed Shrink Bands)

### Technical Specifications
- **Printing system:** Rotogravure (huecograbado) — engraved chrome-plated steel cylinders
- **Maximum colors:** 6 simultaneous colors in register
- **Resolution:** 120 lines per inch (LPI) — comparable to magazine quality
- **Minimum legible type:** 6pt font with perfect readability
- **Ink system:** Solvent-based inks with nitroacrylic resins, specially formulated for shrink films
  - Heat resistant to 130°C (won't bleed during shrinkage)
  - Flexible — stretches with the film without cracking
  - Excellent adhesion to PVC substrate
  - Food-grade for indirect contact
- **Reverse printing:** Inks are printed on the inside surface, sealed behind the transparent film — protects against scuffing, scratching, and chemical abrasion during transport
- **Shrinkage ink loss:** Film loses 3-5% of its shrinkage potential in areas covered by ink (must be compensated in design)
- **Calibres:** 40, 50, 60 micras
- **Print speed:** Up to 300 meters/minute (industrial rotogravure presses)
- **Cylinder ownership:** Client owns the cylinders; stored at no charge
- **Color matching:** Pantone Coated (PMS) exact formula per cylinder

### Printable Content
1. **Brand identity:** Logo, product name, tagline — maximizes the small surface area (15-30mm height)
2. **Traceability data:** Linear barcodes, QR codes, DataMatrix — fixed (same on all pieces) or variable (overprinted digitally in a second pass)
3. **Legal text:** Tamper-evidence statements, sanitary registration numbers, NOM/COFEPRIS/ANMAT required legends
4. **Security elements:** Guilloche patterns, security backgrounds, micro-printing for anti-counterfeiting
5. **Batch/lot information:** Lot number, manufacturing date, expiry date
6. **Multi-language text:** For export products requiring labels in Spanish + English or other languages

### The Rotogravure Process (for content writing)
1. Design is delivered in Adobe Illustrator (.ai) or layered PDF — one layer per ink color, Pantone Coated or CMYK
2. Pre-press: Color proof on actual PVC substrate (not paper, not screen) for client approval
3. Cylinder engraving: Design is laser-engraved into chrome-plated steel cylinders — one cylinder per color
4. Printing: Each cylinder rotates in an ink well; engraved cells fill with ink; ink transfers to substrate under pressure via capillary action; doctor blade removes excess ink
5. Drying: Solvent evaporates between printing stations
6. Slitting and converting: Printed film is slit to width, formed into tubes, and cut to individual seals

### Key Benefits & Selling Points
1. **Complete brand communication on a functional seal** — transforms a tamper-evident feature into a marketing surface
2. **120 LPI resolution** — sharper than flexography, comparable to offset for small-format work
3. **Reverse printing** — inks protected behind film, won't scuff or wear during handling
4. **Cylinder amortization** — first order absorbs cylinder cost; reorders are material + printing only (significant savings on repeat orders)
5. **Multi-SKU cylinder sharing** — common elements (logo, background) on shared cylinders; variable elements (flavor name) on individual cylinders = reduced cylinder costs for product families
6. **Solvent-based ink durability** — resists moisture, chemicals, abrasion throughout product lifecycle
7. **Variable data capability** — digital overprinting adds unique serial numbers, lot codes, or QR codes per piece

### Target Industries
- Pharmaceutical laboratories (OTC, supplements)
- Beverage companies with developed brand design
- Cosmetics and perfumery
- Packaged food with traceability requirements
- Exporters needing multi-language text on seals
- Liquor and spirits brands requiring anti-counterfeiting

## 3. SELLO CON PERFORACION (Perforated Shrink Bands)

### Technical Specifications
- **Material:** PVC termoencogible monocapa
- **Calibres:** 40, 50 micras
- **Perforation methods:**
  - **Mechanical:** Rotating perforation wheel with hardened steel needles — creates consistent hole patterns
  - **Laser:** CO2 or fiber laser head — more precise, enables complex perforation patterns (zigzag, curved paths)
- **Perforation hole diameter:** 0.3mm to 0.5mm
- **Perforation pitch (paso):** 0.5mm to 1.5mm between holes, adjusted by gauge and application
- **Opening force:** 400-600 grams (comfortable finger-pull, no tools required)
- **Perforation orientations:**
  - **Horizontal:** Runs parallel to container mouth, divides seal into upper and lower halves — pull up to remove top portion
  - **Vertical:** Runs top-to-bottom, seal opens like an envelope — pull sideways
- **Perforation styles available:** Straight-line, zigzag, micro-perforation, die-cut windows, curved tear paths
- **Post-shrinkage integrity:** Perforation line remains intact after heat application — does not open during shrinkage
- **Compatible with printing:** Yes — perforation is added as a post-extrusion process, can be applied to already-printed seals
- **Timing in production:** Perforation happens after extrusion and before winding, or after printing

### Key Benefits & Selling Points
1. **Accessibility for all consumers:** Elderly, arthritic patients, children, and people in recovery can open without tools — critical for pharmaceutical compliance
2. **Clean, controlled opening:** Material breaks along the perforation line in a straight, predictable path — no jagged tears, no container damage
3. **Tamper evidence is preserved:** A perforated seal that has been opened shows clear, irreversible evidence — satisfies NOM, COFEPRIS, and FDA 21 CFR 211.132 requirements. The regulation requires visible evidence of tampering, NOT that the seal be impossible to open
4. **Reduced product waste in food:** No damage to container mouth or contents when opening cleanly
5. **Invisible to naked eye:** Micro-perforations are imperceptible before opening — seal looks identical to non-perforated version, maintaining premium appearance
6. **Combinable with all other features:** Can be perforated + printed + colored — full customization stack

### Perforation Technology Deep Dive (for expert content)
- **Mechanical perforation:** A hardened steel cylinder with micro-needles rotates against the film as it passes through the converting line. The needles create clean, consistent holes. Advantages: lower cost, reliable for straight-line perforations. Limitation: less suitable for complex patterns.
- **Laser perforation:** A focused laser beam ablates tiny holes in the film at high speed. Advantages: can create zigzag, curved, or custom patterns; no physical contact with film (no contamination risk); adjustable on-the-fly. Limitation: higher equipment cost.
- **Design coordination:** When combining perforation with printing, the perforation line position must avoid critical design elements (barcodes, legal text, QR codes). This is coordinated before cylinder fabrication.

### Target Industries
- **Pharmaceutical OTC:** Analgesics, supplements, vitamins — accessibility for patients with limited hand dexterity
- **Food in jars:** Sauces, jams, condiments, baby food — clean opening preserves product and container
- **Cosmetics:** Creams, lotions with frequent opening — convenience-focused consumers
- **Baby products:** Safety + ease of opening for parents
- **Products for elderly consumers:** Any product where the end user may have reduced grip strength

### Common Applications
- Supplement bottles with wide-mouth caps
- Sauce jars with twist-off lids
- Cosmetic jars and tubes
- Pharmaceutical vials and dropper bottles
- Children's vitamins and gummies
- Pet supplement containers

## 4. SELLO PARA GARRAFON 20L (Shrink Bands for 20-Liter Water Jugs)

### Technical Specifications
- **Material:** PVC termoencogible alta resistencia (high-resistance grade)
- **Calibres:**
  - 60 micras: for single-use PET garrafones
  - 80 micras: for returnable polycarbonate (PC) garrafones
- **Garrafon dimensions (standard 20L):**
  - Overall height: 496mm
  - Width: 260mm
  - Threaded opening: 56mm diameter
  - Neck external diameter at seal point: varies by manufacturer (typically 65-75mm)
- **Seal dimensions:** Typically 134mm x 70mm (flat lay) for standard neck configurations
- **Encogimiento transversal:** 55-65% to conform to neck geometry
- **Thermal resistance:** Stable up to 70°C (withstands industrial washing cycles)
- **Delivery formats:**
  - Preforma cortada (pre-cut): individual seals cut to exact size, ready to apply
  - Rollo para corte en planta (roll for in-plant cutting): lower unit cost, requires cutting equipment
- **Printing options:** 1 to 8 inks, matte or glossy finishes, in PVC 40 and 50 micras caliber
- **Available finishes:** Transparent, colored (white, blue, green most common), or printed with logo

### PET vs. Polycarbonate Garrafon Differences

| Feature | PET (single-use) | Polycarbonate (returnable) |
|---------|------------------|---------------------------|
| Wall thickness | Thin | Thick |
| Neck diameter | Narrower, more consistent | Wider, varies by manufacturer |
| Usage cycles | 1 (disposable) | 40-60 cycles |
| Washing | None | Industrial: 60-70°C with alkaline detergent |
| Required seal gauge | 60 micras | 80 micras |
| Seal adhesion needs | Standard | High — must not slip during transport |
| Cost sensitivity | Higher (included in product cost) | Lower (amortized over cycles) |

### The Returnable Garrafon Cycle (for expert content)
1. Delivery to customer
2. Water consumption
3. Return of empty garrafon
4. Industrial washing: 60-70°C water with alkaline detergent — old seal must be removed or resist without contaminating
5. Visual inspection
6. Refilling
7. Capping
8. New seal application
9. Repeat 40-60 times

### Regulatory Context
- **NOM-201-SSA1-2015:** Requires that bottled water garrafones must be sealed with either an inviolable cap OR a cap with guarantee seal/band when ready for sale
- **Labeling requirements:** Seal must allow space for lot number and expiry date stamping (rear-side stamping common)
- **Fluoride warning:** If water contains >1.5 mg/L fluoride, label must state: "Este producto no es apto para lactantes y niños menores de siete años"

### Key Benefits & Selling Points
1. **Industrial wash resistance** — 80-micra gauge survives 60-70°C alkaline wash cycles without deformation
2. **Universal neck compatibility** — manufactured to exact neck diameter of each garrafon brand, not generic sizes
3. **Dual format delivery** — pre-cut for small operations, rolls for high-volume plants with integrated cutters
4. **Multi-brand white seal option** — plants filling garrafones for multiple brands can use blank white seals and apply brand-specific adhesive labels on top
5. **Traceability printing** — fill number, cycle count, QR code for production tracking
6. **NOM-201 compliance** — satisfies the regulatory requirement for tamper-evident closure

## 5. SELLO PARA FRASCO FARMACEUTICO (Pharmaceutical Grade PVC Shrink Seals)

### Technical Specifications
- **Material:** PVC grado alimenticio (food/pharmaceutical grade)
- **Plasticizers:** ATBC (acetyl tributyl citrate) for thin gauges; DINCH (diisononylcyclohexane-1,2-dicarboxylate) for flexibility — NO DEHP (bis(2-ethylhexyl) phthalate), which is classified as an endocrine disruptor
- **Stabilizers:** Calcium-zinc based — NO lead, NO cadmium
- **Pigments (if used):** Food-contact approved, free of heavy metals (Pb, Cd, Cr VI)
- **Calibres:** 40, 50 micras
- **Migration compliance:** Meets NOM standards AND FDA 21 CFR 177.1950 (Vinyl chloride-ethylene copolymers for food contact)
- **Application temperature:** 85°C - 120°C
- **Material shelf life:** 12 months from manufacture date, stored at 15-25°C away from direct sunlight
- **Minimum delivered shelf life:** 10 months remaining at time of delivery

### Documentation Package (included with every order)
1. **Ficha técnica del material (Material Technical Data Sheet):** Composition, physical properties, shrinkage test results, application temperature range
2. **Certificado de conformidad del proveedor de resina (Resin Supplier Conformity Certificate):** Global and specific migration test results
3. **Certificado de lote (Lot Certificate):** Lot number, manufacturing date, material expiry date, dimensional quality control results
4. All documents signed by quality department — ready for BPM (Buenas Practicas de Manufactura / GMP) audits

### Regulatory Framework

#### Mexico — COFEPRIS
- **NOM-059-SSA1-2015:** Buenas Practicas de Fabricacion de Medicamentos — governs GMP including packaging material specifications. Requires that packaging material specs include description, code, reference per FEUM (Farmacopea de los Estados Unidos Mexicanos)
- **NOM-072-SSA1-2012:** Etiquetado de medicamentos y remedios herbolarios — labeling requirements for medications
- **NOM-218-SSA1:** Applies to supplement manufacturers
- **EMSF (Expediente Maestro de Sitio de Fabricacion):** Must be updated every 2 years and notified to COFEPRIS if changes occur

#### USA — FDA
- **21 CFR 211.132:** Tamper-evident packaging requirements for OTC human drug products
  - Heat shrink bands must be "securely applied to a portion of the container, usually at the juncture of the cap and container"
  - Must be "cut or torn to open the container and remove the product"
  - "Cannot be worked off and reapplied without visible damage"
  - Cellulose wet shrink seals are explicitly NOT acceptable
- **21 CFR 177.1950:** Material composition requirements for vinyl chloride polymers in food contact

#### Argentina — ANMAT
- Compatible for export to ANMAT-regulated markets

### What "Food Grade" Means for PVC Seals (for expert content)
- PVC polymer itself is inert and safe
- The risk comes from ADDITIVES: plasticizers, stabilizers, pigments
- DEHP (phthalate) — historically the cheapest PVC plasticizer — is an endocrine disruptor, banned for food/pharma contact
- Safe alternatives: ATBC (citrate-based, biodegradable) and DINCH (cyclohexane-based, non-toxic)
- Stabilizers: calcium-zinc replaces traditional lead/cadmium stabilizers
- Migration testing: measures what substances transfer from the packaging material to the product under simulated conditions

### Key Benefits & Selling Points
1. **Complete audit trail** — ficha tecnica + resin certificate + lot certificate with every shipment = audit-ready documentation for COFEPRIS, FDA, ANMAT inspectors
2. **DEHP-free formulation** — uses ATBC/DINCH plasticizers, not the banned phthalates
3. **Heavy-metal-free** — calcium-zinc stabilizers, no lead, cadmium, or hexavalent chromium
4. **Migration-tested** — meets both Mexican NOM and FDA 21 CFR 177.1950 standards
5. **12-month material shelf life** — with minimum 10 months remaining at delivery
6. **All configurations available:** transparent, colored, printed, perforated — all in pharma-grade material
7. **Controlled manufacturing environment** — same production line as food-grade film, temperature and cleanliness controlled

## GENERAL TECHNICAL KNOWLEDGE (Cross-Product)

### PVC vs. PETG vs. OPS — Material Comparison

| Property | PVC | PETG | OPS |
|----------|-----|------|-----|
| Shrinkage rate | 50-65% | 70-78% | 65-75% |
| Cost | Lowest | Highest | Medium |
| Clarity | Good | Excellent | Good |
| Scuff resistance | Moderate | Highest | Moderate |
| Environmental | Contains chlorine; emits HCl when heated improperly | Recyclable; no chlorine | Recyclable |
| Heat resistance | Lower | Highest | Medium |
| Best for | Cost-effective bands and seals | Premium full-body sleeves | Mid-range sleeves |
| Yellowing over time | Possible | No | No |

### Temperature Reference
- Shrinkage onset: 60°C
- Optimal shrinkage: 90-100°C
- Application range: 85°C - 130°C (depending on gauge, speed, and equipment)
- Maximum for industrial wash resistance: 70°C
- Ink heat resistance: up to 130°C

### Standard Gauges and Their Uses
- **40 micras:** Most economical. High-speed lines. Regular cap shapes. Highest volume seller.
- **50 micras:** Intermediate. Caps with slight irregularities. Good cost-performance balance.
- **60 micras:** Pronounced cap shapes. Higher transport resistance. Garrafon PET.
- **80 micras:** Polycarbonate returnable garrafones. Industrial wash resistance.

### Application Methods
1. **Pistola de calor (heat gun):** Manual, for small batches. Temperature 200-300°C at nozzle, distance matters.
2. **Tunel de vapor (steam tunnel):** Most uniform shrinkage. Ideal for irregular shapes. Higher energy cost.
3. **Tunel de aire caliente (hot air tunnel):** Good for standard shapes. Lower energy cost than steam.
4. **Combination heater + blower:** Compact solution for medium-volume lines.
